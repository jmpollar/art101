Banana Bread Recipe Pseudocode

  1. Gather your ingredients:
      3 bananas
        Mash them.
      1/3 cup butter
        Melt it.
      1 teaspoon baking soda
      A pinch of salt
      3/4 cup sugar
      1 large egg
        Beat it.
      1 teaspoon vanilla extract
      1 1/2 cups flour, all-purpose
  2. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C)
  3. Acquire a 9x5 inch loaf pan
      Grease it.
  4. Add the melted butter to the mashed bananas.
      Stir well.
  5. Add the baking soda and salt to the mix.
      Stir.
  6. Add the sugar, beaten egg, and vanilla extract to the mix.
      Stir.
  7. Add the flour.
      Mix until combined.
        DO not overmix.
  8. Pour the batter into the greased loaf pan.
  9. Bake for 50-60 minutes OR until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out without residue.
  10. Remove the banana bread from the oven.
  11. Let it cool in the pan for 5 minutes.
  12. Transfer the bread to a wire rack.
  13. Let the bread cool completely.
      Slice.
      Serve.
